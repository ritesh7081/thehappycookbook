EGG BHURJI

INGREDIENTS
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 3 tsp butter
  • 1 tsp garlic
  • 2 tsp green Chillies
  • 2 tsp ginger
  • 6-7 Curry Leaves
  • 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  • 3 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp coriander stems
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 Eggs
  • Coriander leaves

INSTRUCTION

Take a pan and pour oil in it.Masala Anda Bhurji

Add butter, garlic, green chillies and ginger to the pan.Masala Anda Bhurji

Stir them well until golden brown.

Now mix curry leaves and chopped onions to the pan and saute together.Masala Anda Bhurji

Add salt, turmeric powder, chilli powder, Pav bhaji masala and mix them well.Masala Anda Bhurji

Top it with Coriander stems and chopped tomatoes and mix it in the pan along with other ingredients.Masala Anda Bhurji

Now add the eggs to the pan and whisk them thoroughly until cooked.Masala Anda Bhurji

Pour some butter over and garnish it with coriander leaves.Masala Anda Bhurji

Serve hot with buttered bread.
